## Questions for county planning

1. What is the current zoning district for this parcel?
2. Is a single-family residence permitted by right, conditional use, special exception, or prohibited?
3. Are there overlay districts, floodplain rules, resource protections, scenic corridors, or conservation restrictions?
4. What is the minimum lot size and frontage requirement?
5. Are there setback, height, access, driveway, or road-maintenance requirements?
6. Are manufactured homes, tiny homes, RV living, camping, accessory structures, or ADUs allowed?
7. Are short-term rentals allowed or separately regulated?
8. Is subdivision possible? If so, under what constraints?
9. Are there known code enforcement, permit, or violation records for the parcel?
10. What departments must also approve: septic, well, road/driveway, fire, floodplain, environmental?

## Questions for health/septic department

1. Has the parcel had a perc/septic test?
2. Are soil limitations known?
3. Is minimum acreage required for septic?
4. Are there setbacks from wells, water bodies, slopes, or property lines?
5. Is engineered septic likely required?

## Questions for road/access authority

1. Is the adjacent road public or private?
2. Is it county-maintained?
3. Are driveway permits required?
4. Is year-round access maintained?
5. Are there recorded access/easement issues visible to the agency?
