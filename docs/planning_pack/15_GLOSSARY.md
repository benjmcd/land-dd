# 15 Glossary

## Area
Any parcel, polygon, locality, watershed, corridor, or generated candidate region being analyzed.

## Authority level
A rating of how authoritative a source is: official primary, official secondary, commercial normalized, open community, derived model, user-supplied, unknown.

## Claim
An interpreted assertion derived from evidence.

## Confidence
How much trust the system places in a claim given source authority, freshness, precision, conflicts, and missing data.

## Dataset version
A specific retrieved/published version of a source dataset.

## Dossier
Human-readable report containing evidence, claims, maps, red flags, unknowns, and verification tasks.

## Evidence
A source-linked observation or source-failure record.

## Hard gate
A rule that flags a potentially material blocker before scoring.

## Intent
The purpose for which an area is evaluated.

## Jurisdiction adapter
A module that maps local/country/state-specific legal/source concepts into the common evidence/claim model.

## Source failure
A first-class evidence record indicating that a source was unavailable, unusable, or inconclusive.

## Suitability
How well an area appears to match a specific intent, after hard gates and confidence adjustments.

## Verification task
A concrete action required to verify a claim or unknown, such as ordering title review or calling county planning.
