## Provenance and caveats

- Source version/date: updated state by state; release dates vary
- Update cadence: irregular; tied to state-level research contributions
- Coverage limits: partial US coverage; focuses on single-family residential zoning districts; may not capture agricultural, rural, or mixed-use zoning common in rural NC parcels
- Important: NZA is a **research-derived dataset**, not an official government source; zoning determinations in reports must cite the underlying government ordinance, not NZA
- NC coverage: **unconfirmed** — as of the review date, NC was not confirmed to be in the NZA dataset; Chatham, Buncombe, and Brunswick counties require specific confirmation
- Use-case fit: NZA focuses on single-family residential zoning parameters (minimum lot size, density, permitted uses in residential districts). For rural homestead/agricultural due diligence, the relevant zoning types (A-1, R-A, RA, rural residential, estate) may be partially covered or out of scope.
