## Provenance and caveats

- Source version/date available: Annual Soils Refresh year/date and survey-area metadata where available.
- Source-specific upstream/update cadence: annual; USDA NRCS performs Annual Soils Refresh for public soil survey data and interpretations.
- Terms/source-page review triggers: Review source terms/pages earlier if USDA/NRCS terms/source pages change.
- Geographic coverage limits: Most U.S. areas, territories, commonwealths, and island nations served by USDA-NRCS; availability varies by survey area and product.
- Precision/scale limits: SSURGO map scales range from 1:12,000 to 1:63,360; map units and interpretations are screening data, not parcel-specific field tests.
- Known caveats to store with evidence: Soil/septic/ag proxy only; not a substitute for site-specific soil test, septic/percolation test, engineering report, wetland delineation, permitting decision, or legal/buildability conclusion.
- Required attribution text: Soil Survey Staff, Natural Resources Conservation Service, United States Department of Agriculture. Web Soil Survey. Available online at https://websoilsurvey.nrcs.usda.gov/. Accessed [date].
- Fields that must not be exposed: Do not use USDA/NRCS symbols or logos in a way that implies endorsement; preserve any marked third-party notices.
- Source failure / no-data behavior: Persist source-failure evidence when WSS/SSURGO has no spatial data for the requested area, access fails, query bounds are too large, or data/metadata are malformed.
- Reviewer notes: Approved for MVP soil/septic/ag screening use with restrictions; connector implementation still must define bounded AOI/survey-area behavior and report caveat mapping.
