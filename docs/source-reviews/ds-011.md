## Scope and status

DS-011 covers county assessor/tax records: assessed value, tax year, situs address, and related fields. Access method, schema, and license terms vary by county — ranging from REST APIs to bulk CSV downloads to FOIA requests. For the private MVP:

- **No live connector is implemented.** County assessor data is intentionally excluded from the Chatham County GIS connector (DS-010), which queries only PIN, ACRES, and ZONING.
- **Status: NOT_EVALUATED.** The connector gate for DS-011 has not been opened. No machine-queryable assessor endpoint has been selected or reviewed.
- **Review deferred** until a specific county assessor endpoint is selected and access terms are reviewed.

## Why NOT_EVALUATED

- Assessor data varies significantly by county in schema, access method, update cadence, and license.
- Owner name, owner address, and assessed value are fields with privacy implications in some jurisdictions; a per-county field policy decision is required before integration.
- The private MVP parcel screening goal is met by DS-010 (geometry + PIN + ZONING) without assessor data for the initial build.
- Official county web sources exist for all three private-MVP counties, but each still needs endpoint-level access review, field-policy approval, and connector design before DS-011 can be promoted.
