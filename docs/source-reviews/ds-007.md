# Data Source Review: DS-007 BLM MLRS

## Source identity

- Source registry ID: DS-007
- Source name: BLM MLRS (Mineral and Land Records System)
- Organization: Bureau of Land Management (BLM), U.S. Department of the Interior
- Public GIS catalog: https://www.blm.gov/services/geospatial/GISData
- Reviewed service: https://gis.blm.gov/nlsdb/rest/services/Mining_Claims/MiningClaims/MapServer
- Reviewed layer: https://gis.blm.gov/nlsdb/rest/services/Mining_Claims/MiningClaims/MapServer/1
- Domain: Mineral/land records
- Geography: United States federal/public-land mining-claim record context; does not cover private mineral estates
- Source type / authority level: Public official / official_primary for federal mining-claim records
- MVP priority: Later
- Date reviewed: 2026-06-11
- Review owner: operator
- Review status: approved-with-restrictions

## Authority and live endpoint check

- BLM's geospatial catalog states that BLM data can be explored, viewed, and downloaded through its geospatial hub in formats including GeoJSON and GeoServices.
- The reviewed Mining Claims MapServer identifies the dataset as mining-claim cases derived from MLRS legal land descriptions and mapped using PLSS.
- The reviewed service exposes separate `Case`, `Active Mining Claims`, and `Closed Mining Claims` layers. This connector uses only layer 1, `Active Mining Claims`.
- Layer 1 is an ArcGIS Feature Layer with polygon geometry, JSON/GeoJSON/PBF query support, and a documented max record count of 2000.
- Layer fields used by the connector are `OBJECTID`, `CSE_NR`, `LEG_CSE_NR`, `CSE_NAME`, `CSE_DISP`, `CSE_TYPE_NR`, `BLM_PROD`, `QLTY`, and `RCRD_ACRS`.
- Live bounded probes on 2026-06-11 returned HTTP 200. A Buncombe/NC-adjacent bbox returned zero features. A Nevada mineral-area bbox returned active claim features and `exceededTransferLimit=true`; the connector therefore fails closed on any `exceededTransferLimit=true` response instead of treating partial records as complete authority.

## Provenance and caveats

- Source version/date: live MLRS-derived BLM Mining Claims service as retrieved at request time.
- Update cadence: continuous/current-effective for screening purposes.
- Coverage limits: federal/public-land active mining-claim context only. It does not determine private mineral rights, state-owned mineral rights, patented/fee mineral status, title status, claim-boundary precision, or absence of mineral severance.
- Geometry limits: BLM states geospatial representations are derived from legal land descriptions and PLSS mapping; some records may be approximate, missing geometry, or mapped only to section/county-level context.
- Required caveat: BLM MLRS Active Mining Claims geospatial context only; no private mineral-rights, claim-boundary, title, mine-hazard, resource-value, extraction, environmental-liability, buildability, appraisal, lending, insurance, or investment conclusion.
- Source failure behavior: emit SOURCE_FAILURE evidence on HTTP/network/JSON/service/malformed responses and on `exceededTransferLimit=true`; record failure_reason and attempted_url.

Out of scope for this connector:

- Closed claims, all-case MLRS history, notices/plans, leases, or other BLM land records.
- Private mineral estate, title, deed/easement, patented claim, severed-mineral, or legal-access determinations.
- Mine hazards, resource value, extraction feasibility, environmental liability, buildability, appraisal, lending, insurance, or investment suitability.
