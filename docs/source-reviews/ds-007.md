## Provenance and caveats

- Source version/date: MLRS records updated as mineral leases, notices of intent, and mining claims are filed
- Update cadence: continuous (as federal filings occur)
- Coverage limits: **federal lands ONLY**; does not resolve private mineral rights, state-owned mineral rights, or fee-simple (patented) mining claims that have reverted to private ownership
- Required caveats: BLM MLRS does not confirm absence of private mineral rights; a title search is required to establish full mineral estate status; this source only screens for open/active federal mining claims and leases in the vicinity
- Source failure behavior: emit SOURCE_FAILURE evidence on any HTTP/network error; record failure_reason
