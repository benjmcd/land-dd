## Provenance and caveats

- Source version/date: NWS point/zone data is updated as forecast zones and offices change (infrequent)
- Update cadence: NWS forecast zones and office assignments are relatively stable; check on major office reorganizations
- Coverage limits: NOAA NWS API covers US contiguous states and territories; available for all three MVP NC counties
- Required caveats: NOAA NWS forecast zone coverage only; this connector provides administrative weather service metadata (forecast office, zone classification, timezone, radar station) but does NOT provide historical climate normals, frost dates, growing season length, hardiness zone, or agricultural risk data; for homestead and agricultural planning consult NOAA NCEI 1991–2020 climate normals and the USDA Plant Hardiness Zone Map
- Source failure behavior: emit SOURCE_FAILURE evidence on any HTTP/network error; record failure_reason
