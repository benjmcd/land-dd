## Provenance and caveats

- Source version/date available: CAMA data-lag from county GIS refresh cycle; no per-record effective-date field in the ArcGIS query response.
- Update cadence or freshness class: dynamic; county GIS service is live but CAMA lag applies.
- Geographic coverage limits: Chatham County, NC only (private-MVP scope); other counties are NOT_EVALUATED in this registry row.
- Precision/scale limits: Approximate parcel geometry; not a survey; boundaries may not match recorded plat.
- Known caveats to store with evidence: Parcel screening only; not a survey, title determination, or buildability finding; boundaries may not match recorded plat; CAMA data subject to county refresh lag.
- Required attribution text: "Chatham County GIS / CAMA" with retrieval date; source URL per query.
- Fields that must not be exposed: Owner name, owner address, assessed value, sale history — not queried by connector.
- Source failure / no-data behavior: Connector emits SOURCE_FAILURE evidence when the ArcGIS endpoint returns an error, times out, or returns no features.
- Reviewer notes: Approved for Chatham County MVP parcel screening with restrictions; raw data export and AI use remain restricted; field policy (no owner/value) is enforced in the connector, not the registry.
