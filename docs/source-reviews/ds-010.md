> "Buncombe County assumes no legal responsibility for this information. Data are presented using the North Carolina State Coordinate System 1983 North American Datum. Horizontal measurement units are in feet.
>
> Buncombe County shall not be held liable for any errors in the GIS data. This includes errors of omission, commission, errors concerning the content of the data, and relative and positional accuracy of the data. Source information used for these data may have been collected at different scales, times or definitions, resulting in inconsistencies among features represented together on this map. Primary sources from which these data were compiled must be consulted for verification of information contained in the data. Buncombe County will not re-distribute data developed from other organizations. Parcel data was prepared for the inventory of real property found within this jurisdiction, and is compiled from recorded deeds, plats, and other public records and data. ## Provenance and caveats

- Source version/date available: Buncombe: daily refresh (M–F); Chatham: weekly via Python scripting per ArcGIS service metadata; Brunswick: periodic county update (last noted 04/01/2026 on download page).
- Source-specific upstream/update cadence: dynamic (all three counties); Chatham service explicitly states weekly refresh.
- Terms/source-page review triggers: Review source terms/pages earlier if any county's service terms, access model, or data availability changes.
- Geographic coverage limits: Buncombe County NC, Chatham County NC, and Brunswick County NC only (private-MVP scope); other counties are NOT_EVALUATED in this registry row.
- Precision/scale limits: Approximate parcel geometry for all three counties; not survey-grade; boundaries may not match recorded plat. All three county disclaimers confirm data is compiled from deeds, plats, and public records — primary sources must be consulted for verification.
- Known caveats to store with evidence: Parcel screening only; NOT a survey, title determination, legal access finding, or buildability determination; boundaries may not match recorded plat; county data subject to refresh lag; primary sources (deeds, plats, county records) must be consulted for verification.
- Required attribution text:
  - Buncombe: "Buncombe County GIS" with retrieval date and source URL
  - Chatham: "Chatham County GIS / CAMA" with retrieval date and source URL
  - Brunswick: "Brunswick County GIS" with retrieval date and source URL
- Fields that must not be exposed: Owner name, owner address, assessed value, sale history — not queried by any connector.
- Source failure / no-data behavior: Connector emits SOURCE_FAILURE evidence when a county endpoint returns an error, times out, or returns no features.
- Reviewer notes: Approved for all three MVP counties with restrictions. All three counties operate under NC G.S. § 132-1 public records law and publish GIS data as open/public with informational-only disclaimers. No API key required for any county. Chatham County's full standalone terms-of-use page was not confirmed — this review records the disclaimer language sourced from the county portal. Buncombe's verbatim disclaimer was confirmed from the NC state GICC LGC GIS Disclaimer Examples PDF. Brunswick's verbatim disclaimer was confirmed from the county Data Download page. Raw data export, resale, and AI use remain restricted for all three counties.
