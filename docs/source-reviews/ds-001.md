## Provenance and caveats

- Source version/date available: Varies by National Map product/layer; product metadata should be captured per retrieval.
- Source-specific upstream/update cadence: dynamic; The National Map uses products, services, viewers, web map services, direct downloads, and staged data.
- Terms/source-page review triggers: Review source terms/pages earlier if USGS terms/source pages change.
- Geographic coverage limits: United States and territories, with product/layer availability varying by theme and area.
- Precision/scale limits: Accuracy and currentness vary by product, source layer, scale, collection date, and metadata; USGS notes no single accuracy statement covers all US Topo maps because source data vary.
- Known caveats to store with evidence: Physical screening only; terrain/slope/base-map context is not a survey, title, parcel-boundary, legal-access, water-rights, wetland-jurisdiction, buildability, insurance, lending, or appraisal determination.
- Required attribution text: Credit U.S. Geological Survey / The National Map, with source URL, retrieval date, product/layer, and source date/version when available.
- Fields that must not be exposed: Do not use the trademarked USGS identifier or official marks in a way that implies endorsement; preserve any marked third-party copyright notices.
- Source failure / no-data behavior: Persist source-failure evidence when National Map services/downloads fail, return no data for a requested layer/area, or omit needed metadata.
- Reviewer notes: Approved for MVP physical screening/base-layer use with restrictions. Product-specific metadata and marked third-party exceptions must be checked at retrieval time.
