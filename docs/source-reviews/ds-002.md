## Provenance and caveats

- Source version/date available: Effective NFHL data and FIRM panel/effective-date layers where exposed.
- Update cadence or freshness class: current-effective; FEMA states new/revised data are added continually.
- Geographic coverage limits: NFHL digital data does not cover every location; not all FIRMs have GIS data.
- Precision/scale limits: FEMA notes horizontal-control/base-map accuracy requirements for official purposes.
- Known caveats to store with evidence: Screening only; not all flood risk; not final legal, insurance, lending, or official NFIP determination.
- Required attribution text: Cite FEMA source endpoint/page and retrieval date; state the product is not endorsed by FEMA and FEMA/Federal Government cannot vouch for derived analysis after retrieval.
- Fields that must not be exposed: FEMA/DHS logos or seals without written authorization.
- Source failure / no-data behavior: Persist source-failure evidence when NFHL service has no coverage, request limits are hit, or retrieval fails.
- Reviewer notes: Approved for MVP flood screening with restrictions; raw data export remains restricted.
